Lettuce Salad Recipe

Description

A salad you can make when you just give up because you're tired and feeling lazy.

Ingredients

  • Head of lettuce
  • Cold water

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Wash off the lettuce thoroughly with the water.
  3. Cut the lettuce head in half. Set the halves on a plate.
  4. Eat.
