Buttermilk Pancake Recipe

Description

An easy to make homemade pancake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoons salt
  • 3 cups buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Mix the buttermilk, milk, eggs, and melted butter in a separate large bowl.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan to medium heat. If you drip water onto the surface and it sizzles it's ready.
  4. Carefully pour the bowl with the wet mixture into the bowl with the dry mixture. Mix until it's just blended together. It's fine if it's a little lumpy.
  5. Pour approximately half a cup of the batter onto the griddle or frying pan. Cook for 2 minutes or until bubbles appear on the surface. Flip with a spatula and cook until the other side is also browned.
  6. Repeat the previous step until there is no more batter left. Enjoy.
